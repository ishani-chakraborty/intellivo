What We Do:

Facilitate conversations between individuals who may have differing views.

It's important to hear differing views and talk about it so that we can grow as individuals in a changing society. By matching people together by how different they are, we are showing how possible it is to have important conversations with people who are not exactly like you.

space

How We Do it:

By implementating a clustering algorithm, we are able to to create a simulation of echo chambers from a questionnaire that users complete following registration. We then match people in differing echo chambers and provide them a chat room and prompt to have an intelligent conversation!
